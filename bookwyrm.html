The Bookwyrm

I found myself more interested in the intricacies of soul-power as of late. For whatever reason, the professors have deemed it an "unethical" subject and have banished all mention of it to the restricted section of the library.

Professor Cogwell seems to be adamant in his refusal to let me take out the books, not for a lack of effort on my part. Seems I will have to take the more "unscholarly" approach to get in.

There seems to be some sort of creature guarding the section, there was a mound of paper that looked like it was breathing. What is sleeping under it, I do not know, but I am determined to find out.

Upon closer inspection, it appears to be some sort of dragon sitting on a pile of books. Its body is made out of paper, and is covered in text from various different literatures. I can recognize a few of the passages.

I've located the book on soul-power I was looking for, but its on the dragon's hoard. I will have to be quiet if I hope to sneak it from the beast.

I got the book, but not without getting hit by its breath. It looked like some form of ink, but based on the damage its done to my leg, I would guess it is corrosive in some manner. It is hunting me down tirelessly. I hope my leg can recover before it find me.

THIEVES WILL NOT BE TOLERATED IN MY LIBRARY

-Journal of an Unknown Nightvale Student & The Bookwyrm
